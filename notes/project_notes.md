What specific metrics do I need to use for each category?

Passing:
    Find the difference in average passing attempts, yds, and % of plays that are passes in wins vs losses for each team.(% of plays = attemps/total plays)

    Find difference in average comp%, y/a, y/comp, ay/a, and qb rating for each team in wins vs losses for each team. 

Rushing:
    Find difference in attempts, % of plays, % of scores in wins vs losses. (% of scores = rush_td/tot_tds)

    Find difference in y/a, rush_yds, td per game( tot_td/17) in wins vs losses

Special Teams:
    Average fgs made and attempted in wins vs losses

    Difference in xp percentage in wins vs losses

    % of points from fg in wins vs losses.

Sloppy Play:
    Offense: Find difference in number of tovs, ints, fbls, penalties, yds from penalties, and sacks allowed in wins vs. losses. 

    Defense: Difference in # of penalties, yds from penalties by opposing team in wins vs. losses.

    Yds per play - penalty yds per play

Defense:
    Rush attempts vs yds allowed in wins vs losses.
    Pass attempts vs yds allowed in wins vs losses.
    Opp team 3rd down conversion rates in wins vs losses. 
    Difference in type of play that earned opp team 1st down. 
    TOV caused gap in wins vs losses.
    YDS per play allowed(are teams losing by big chunk plays or are they getting walked down?)

Downs Play:
    Difference in average 1st downs, 3rd down conversions, 4th down conversions in wins vs. losses
    
    3rd and 4th down conversion rates in wins vs losses

    4th down attempts in wins vs losses.

    % of 1st downs gained by penalties vs. rush vs. pass.
